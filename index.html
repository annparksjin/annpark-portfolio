Working Style

User Manual

The best ways to communicate with me

  • Text and email are usually the fastest ways to reach me.
  • For urgent work, a clear subject or priority note helps me respond quickly.

The ways I like to receive feedback

  • I value direct feedback with concrete suggestions and examples.
  • I see feedback as a useful part of learning and improving.

Things I struggle with

  • It is harder to do my best work when the bigger purpose of a task is unclear.
  • Heavy critique without encouragement can slow my momentum.

Conditions I like to work in

  • I focus best in a quiet environment with room to concentrate.
  • I often use earbuds to reduce distractions, but interruptions are okay when needed.

Things I need

  • I enjoy exchanging ideas and hearing different perspectives.
  • Clear deadlines and short resets between major tasks help me sustain quality.

Other things to know about me

  • I ask follow-up questions when I want to make sure the work is right.
  • I care a lot about steady growth and doing better work over time.
