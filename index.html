Communications

The best ways to communicate with me

  • Text and email are the quickest ways to get a response from me.
  • If something is time-sensitive, flagging it clearly helps me prioritize quickly.

Feedback

The ways I like to receive feedback

  • I see all feedback as a learning opportunity and a way to better understand expectations.
  • I prefer direct feedback with specific suggestions and examples.

Struggles

Things I struggle with

  • I struggle when I don’t understand the purpose or bigger picture of a task. Clear goals and context help me perform at my best.
  • Too much critique without encouragement can drain my energy. I thrive when feedback includes both strengths and areas to grow.

Conditions

Conditions I like to work in

  • I work best in a quiet environment with personal space to concentrate.
  • I often wear earbuds to block out distractions, but please feel free to interrupt me if needed.

Needs

Things I need

  • I love exchanging ideas and hearing different perspectives. It sparks my creativity.
  • I need short mental resets between major tasks to recharge and maintain quality.
  • Clear deadlines help me manage my time and prioritize effectively.

Other Things

Other things to know about me

  • If something feels off or unclear, I may ask follow-up questions, not to challenge, but to make sure I deliver my best work.
  • I’m always trying to grow, improve, and become better than I was yesterday.
